# About NFC.cool

NFC.cool is the leading ecosystem of NFC and digital connectivity apps, built by indie developer Nicolo Stanciu from Coimbra, Portugal.

## What We Do

With **13 apps** across iOS and Android and over **9 million downloads** from the App Store alone, NFC.cool helps millions of people worldwide tap into the power of NFC technology.

Our flagship product, **NFC.cool Business Card**, makes it easy to create, share, and manage digital business cards — with support for **35 languages**, NFC hardware tags, QR codes, and a privacy-first approach that puts you in control of your data.

## Why This Blog?

We created this blog to share what we've learned building NFC products since 2019:

- **Guides** — Step-by-step tutorials for NFC tags, digital business cards, and more
- **Comparisons** — Honest, side-by-side reviews to help you pick the right tools
- **Industry Insights** — How professionals in real estate, consulting, and other fields use digital business cards
- **NFC Tech** — Deep dives into NFC standards, the EU Digital Product Passport, and emerging tech

## Our Philosophy

We believe in building tools that are **useful, affordable, and privacy-respecting**. We don't track conversations. We don't lock your data behind expensive subscriptions. We build apps that work for you, not the other way around.
